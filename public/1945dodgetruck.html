1945 W3C dodge truck January 2020

  • custom oak wood bed
  • Dodge Hemi 5.7 crate engine
  • TR6060 6 speed manual transmission
  • refinished in black and chrysler maroon
  • Custom 4WD Chassis
  • Boese engineering Instrument cluster
  • Vintage air conditioning system
  • 20 inch Detroit Mobsteel wheels

with this custom build, we combined modern day technologies into a 1945 war era W3C dodge truck, we began with a custom F series 4 x 4 frame, installed a dodge Hemi powertrain assembly and replaced the electrical system with a modern day system. Boese engineering gauges, Vintage air and custom upholstery complete the Interior, we then built a custom oak wood bed stained in a Old Masters Vintage Burgundy. we made a custom rear bumper and installed 20 inch Detroit Mobsteel Steel Wheels on BF Goodrich all terrain radial TA Tires. custom Peterbilt inspired rear fenders formed from stainless steel. Installed twin high speed spal cooling fans and a custom cooling module keep this heavy duty truck cool and efficient. all of the work was completed in house at gorilla. this project took a year to complete and is surely one of a kind.
