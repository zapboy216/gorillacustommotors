Project: 1955 Ford Thunderbird August 2016

Gorilla Custom Motors is proud to present this restored to Concours 1955 Ford Thunderbird, which was produced on June 18, 1955 at the Ford Dearborn factory. Painted in Snowshoe White and optioned with the 292 c.i. V8 engine mated to a Ford0matic Automatic Transmission. This bird comes with two tops; a black canvass soft top, and a matching white hardtop with the favorite side porthole windows. It also comes with a storage stand and cover for the hardtop. A custom full car cover and a 6V battery maintainer is also included. We installed a protective clear film over the rear deck to protect the paint finish when the tops are on the car (see pictures.)
