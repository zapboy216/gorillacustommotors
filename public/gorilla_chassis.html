THE GORILLA CHASSIS

With over 20 years experience in the chassis industry, our Shop draws upon real world performance, Whether it's for performance, custom or comfort, our chassis and suspension systems are designed to perform at the highest levels of race and street performance.

The frames that we build are primarily 2-inch by 4-inch rectangular box tubing With heavier and longer wheelbase cars we will actually use 4-inch by 4-inch box tubing that’s even more rigid, with either .120-inch or .180-inch wall thickness. The weight of the vehicle and how it’s going to be driven both factor into that design process.

We specialize in T.I.G. welding of most metals. We weld aluminum, stainless steel, and steel in a variety of thicknesses ranging from sheet metal to thick metal plate. we specialize in all aspects of custom chassis and suspension work. We are a South Florida full fabrication center that can not only build you a race-winning chassis, but a ground pounding ProTouring car as well

Gorilla also specializes in custom suspension for your daily driver or show car or truck. We can build and install most suspension systems, and lift kits, and we can also tangle with full air-ride suspension systems. Our techs can build you a complete suspension setup that not only performs well, but is also fun to drive.

No job is too small or too big - from the partial restoration services for the do it yourselfers to the complete turnkey auto restoration. We invite you to check out our other pages showing some of the past automobile restorations completed at Gorilla Custom Motors.
