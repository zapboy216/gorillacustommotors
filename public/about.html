We are a dedicated group of automotive experts, a classic car dealer, a restoration shop and an automotive repair facility. We have been restoring, sourcing and supplying quality Florida classics to customers around the world. We have extensive contacts throughout the Florida car community and can usually locate any car that is not in our inventory.

Quality, fit, and finish to the smallest detail is our reputation when repairing or building your classic dream ride. There is specific criteria for fit and finish, at the highest level, so that your ride stands out from the rest. When looking for a restoration or a repair facility, you want to have the best of the best for a long lasting project.
