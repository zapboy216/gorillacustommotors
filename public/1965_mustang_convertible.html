Project: 1965 Mustang Convertible 2015

This 1965 Mustang Convertible is finished in a tri color Le Mans blue with a white leather pony interior. It has a modern air suspension, 4 wheel disc brakes, automatic transmission, 9 inch rear end with 3.70:1 gear set. It has a positive traction differential that delivers power to 18 x11 American Racing torque thrust wheels on 275/40/18 BF Goodrich radial TAs that reside in custom larger rear wheel housings.

Just turn the key, pump the gas once - the car fires up and is ready to roll. This Mustang is a great cruiser and a cinch to drive with the modern suspension, power steering, power brakes and Vintage air conditioning.

  • Body: 1965 Mustang Convertible
  • Color:Lemans blue (basecoat/clearcoat PPG) See our complete painting process here at southern polyurethanes
  • Frame and Metal: Custom full frame by Chassis Engineering, larger rear wheel housings by Detroit Speed that added 2.5 inches more in each rear wheel well. Complimented with a custom 4 point roll bar
  • Interior: Custom Pony genuine white leather, dark blue carpeting and a factory Mustang center console
  • Air Conditioning: Vintage Air Gen-IV Surefit System.
  • Engine:The Original A code 289 4V, with oversize valves in custom ported and polished factory cylinder heads, a ford street racing camshaft and double timing chain and gearset, with 10.5:1 Pistons, this engine has been blueprinted and perfectly balanced within a .5 gram, Ford Motorsports dyno results put this engine at 290hp @5500 rpm.
  • Fuel: Holley 750 double pumper, carter mechanical pump
  • Exhaust: Dual exhaust with flowmasters and 4 inch custom tailpipes.
  • Transmission: C4 automatic completely renewed, Red Devil clutches and steels, performance valve body , and the shift timing has been tuned perfectly.
  • Brakes: Wilwood power 4 wheel disc
  • Differential: Offset 9 inch, 3.70:1 gearset, positive traction, strange axles
  • Suspension: Height adjustable Air Suspension by Air ride technologies, we did away with the Leaf spring suspension and Installed a 4 link rear suspension, with Air Ride Tech's level 3 shock waves air suspension kit combined with a full global west negative roll front suspension system.
  • Steering:Flaming river Stainless steel tilt steering column, 19:1 quick ratio power steering gear, complete moog steering system linkage.
  • Wheels and Tires: American Racing torque thrust on BF Goodrich Radial TAs
    • Rear:275/40/17 on 17x11 wheels
    • Front: 225/45/17 on 17x8 wheels
  • Convertible top: New Haartz Pinpoint Stafast vinyl top with all new hydraulics and electric pump.
  • Audio: Classic retro sound by Luna
  • Extensive Chrome Renewal: All chrome parts new or rechromed
  • Custom Finished Trunk: Fine velour blue
  • Soundproofing: FatMat Sound Control.
